Overview

Purpose

To complete this course and display some of my art work in a portfolio. At least, that's what I am hoping to do. Who knows, maybe I can use this for a personal portfolio page like my LDS Artist buddies are doing.

Audience

The site is intended for anyone who loves art. In particular, my art focuses on the beauty that God created when he made bodies for his spirit children, here on earth. Like Preseident Nelson, I am amazed at the wonder that life is, with all of its complexity and beauty.
