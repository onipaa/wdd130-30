Family

I have fun with my family. This year, we decided to plow under our entire back yard (about 1/3 acre) and plant a huge garden. We only got half of the work done this year and plan to finish it by early Summer 2023. Maintaining a garden gives us a safe place, far away from the hussle and bustle of every day life, to reflect, and to help provide for ourselves and our neighbors. Our hope is that our neighbors will join us in an effort to bring our community together and have fun eating some really good food.
